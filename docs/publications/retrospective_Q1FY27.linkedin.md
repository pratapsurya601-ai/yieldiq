Q1FY27 retrospective — how YieldIQ's model actually did.

This quarter, our DCF model flagged 47 Indian stocks as "undervalued" (margin of safety > 30%). Ninety days later, 55.3% of those calls outperformed the Nifty 500. Mean return: +12.4%. Median: +9.8%. Hit rate (positive return): 63.8%.

Top wins: POWERGRID (+38.2%), BHARTIARTL (+31.7%), SUNPHARMA (+27.4%).
Misses we own: ZOMATO (-18.3%), PAYTM (-14.2%), VEDL (-9.5%).

Why publish the misses? Because survivorship bias is the oldest trick in this industry, and we'd rather lose your trust honestly than keep it dishonestly. Our biggest lesson this quarter is the same one every quarter: a wide margin of safety doesn't protect you from the macro tape, but it does keep the median honest.
